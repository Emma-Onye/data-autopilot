Good questions

Honest answers.

Is this really free? +
The core app — tracking, alerts, one-tap fixes — is free forever. A Pro tier (₦500–₦1,500/month) adds auto-optimization and deeper prediction. You only ever pay when you've already seen savings.
Can you see my chats / photos / banking apps? +
No. We use Android's built-in data metering APIs — they tell us how much data an app used, never what was in it. Your banking app, your WhatsApp chats, your photos are invisible to us. Zero content access is a hard rule, not a marketing line.
iPhone? +
Android first. Nigeria is 90%+ Android, and iOS doesn't give apps enough control to actually fix data waste. Join the waitlist anyway — if enough iPhone users ask, we'll plan it.
When does it launch? +
Closed beta starts May 2026 for Nigerian Android users on the waitlist. Public release follows in Q3 2026.
How do you make money if it's free? +
Three ways — (1) Pro upgrades for automation and deeper prediction, (2) B2B dashboards for schools, hostels, and offices that want to control staff/student data costs, and (3) savings-based pricing for heavy users ("we saved you ₦3,000, pay ₦300"). No ads. Ever. Ads would destroy the whole point.
Who's building this? +
A Nigerian-founded team that's tired of watching ₦5,000 data bundles vanish in 3 days. We built Data Vantage because no one else would.
